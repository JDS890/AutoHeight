# Loading OBJs
Load OBJs using the GUI.
Expand treeview OBJs to view their objects and groups.
All treeview meshes are used in height calculations, unless hidden with the hide button.

# Spreadsheet highlighting
Highlight rows from adjacent XYZ columns, then press Ctrl + Alt + P.
The bottom-right highlighted cell must be the active cell.
The active cell is the highlighted cell entered if you break the selection by typing.
